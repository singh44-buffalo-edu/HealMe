HealMeNow Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 21, 2026

HealMeNow is a personal health-record app designed around one principle: your health data belongs to you and stays on infrastructure you own.

What we collect

Nothing. The developer of HealMeNow operates no servers, runs no analytics, embeds no trackers or third-party SDKs, and receives no data of any kind from the app — health-related or otherwise.

Where your data goes

The app connects exclusively to a Medplum health-record server that you install and operate yourself (for example on your own computer at home). You choose the server address at sign-in. Your medications, dose logs, check-ins, vitals, documents, and every other record are stored only there and on your device's local cache — never with the developer, never with any third party by default.

Apple Health

If — and only if — you turn on the optional Apple Health sync, the app reads the measurements you authorize (such as steps, heart rate, sleep, weight, blood pressure) and stores them in your own server. Apple Health data is never written back, never sent to the developer, and never shared with anyone else. You can revoke access at any time in iOS Settings.

Optional AI features

AI features (such as health-review summaries) are disabled until you configure a provider yourself. If you choose a cloud AI provider and supply your own API key, the specific data used for that feature is sent to that provider under your direct relationship with them — the app clearly discloses each such boundary before any data leaves your infrastructure, and a fully local (on-premises) AI option is offered. The developer is not a party to that traffic and never sees it.

Notifications

Optional reminder notifications are routed via Apple's push service using an opaque device token. Notification payloads contain no medical content — no medication names, no health values.

Data retention and deletion

Because the developer holds none of your data, there is nothing for the developer to retain or delete. Your records live in your own server and can be exported or erased by you at any time. Signing out of the app clears its local session; deleting the app removes its on-device cache.

Children

HealMeNow is not directed at children and does not knowingly process children's data.
